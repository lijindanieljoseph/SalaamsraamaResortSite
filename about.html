Welcome To Our Hotel

Salaams Raama wants to provide you the best experience during the stay. It does not matter the type of stay you choose, we provide all the basic amenities. Our heritage eco village does not want require you to be void of luxury and conveniences just because you’re closer to nature.

We understand the restless soul inside everyone, who wants to go out and enjoy the wilderness. At Salaams Raama, we present you with a variety of activities to choose from, adding value to the experience you have in the eco heritage village.

There are many things to do in Salaams like;

  • Family Fun -- If you are visiting Salaams Raama with your family, we have some awesome family-oriented activities to give you. You can explore a variety of activities such as culinary exploration, agriculture, farming and the various options for trekking. Families love it all the time
  • Trekking -- Do you want to explore the wilderness of Ramakkalmedu and Idukki? Salaams Raama has arranged some of the best trekking experiences you would ever have in your life. From the ethnic stay to the meadows of our mother nature, that’s a journey you would love.
  • Spa -- When you are down on your adventurous spirit and tired, make sure you check out our spa services. Managed and overseen by experienced professionals. Our spa area will be the right happy place for your mind and the body — making you ready for the next adventure ahead.
  • Dining -- Thanks to our in-house restaurant, Salaams Raama can always offer you the best food, rich and authentic. It doesn’t matter if you want something unique or something continental, the Salaams Raama restaurant has quite some options for you.
  • Fitness & Gym -- Salaams Raama also understands the importance of maintaining health and fitness during vacation. In order to help with that, we have set up a full-fledged gym and fitness center at Salaams Raama. With this, you’ll never run out of energy.
