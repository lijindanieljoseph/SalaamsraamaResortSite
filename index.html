Welcome to Salaams Raama

A Complete Heritage Eco Village

Situated at Ramakkalmedu, an amazingly serene hamlet in Idukki, Salaams Raama Heritage Eco Village is more than a simple destination for your leisure time.

It provides you an opportunity to go back in time and enjoy the beauty and simplicity of the old times. Designed to offer the most nature-esque experience, this Heritage Eco Village is more than what meets the eye and offers an experience of a lifetime.
